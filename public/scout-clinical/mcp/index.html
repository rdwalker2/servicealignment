Both Platforms Have AI. The Question Is: What Kind?

ClearCompany and Teamtailor both use AI in their platforms today. The difference is in how each is building what comes next.

🤖

What both platforms already do: Both ClearCompany and Teamtailor have AI built into the product — resume parsing, candidate matching, job description writing, screening recommendations. This isn't new. Teamtailor has shipped its integrated Copilot since 2023, covering everything from AI-generated job ads to candidate summaries to smart screening.

🔒

ClearCompany's next step — Closed Agents: ClearCompany is building 5 proprietary AI agents that run inside their platform. You use ClearCo's agents, on ClearCo's models, at ClearCo's pace. It's a closed system — powerful if they build exactly what you need, limiting if they don't.

🔓

Teamtailor's next step — Open Protocol: Teamtailor took a different approach. On top of the Copilot that's already built into every workflow, they added MCP (Model Context Protocol) — an open standard that lets any AI tool you already use (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) connect directly to your ATS data. We built our own AI, and we know when to partner with the industry leaders for the best result.

Side-by-Side: What's New From Each Platform

Both have existing AI. Here's how each is expanding it.

ClearCompany AI Agents
  • Existing AI: Resume parsing, candidate matching, basic screening — standard ATS features
  • What's new: 5 proprietary AI agents (Sourcing, Screening, Scheduling, Engagement, Compliance)
  • Agents run on ClearCo's models inside ClearCo's platform
  • Pricing unclear — consumption-based with no published cap
  • You're limited to what ClearCo decides to build
  • When a better AI model launches, you wait for ClearCo to adopt it
Teamtailor Copilot + MCP
  • Existing AI (Copilot, since 2023): AI job ads, candidate summaries, smart screening, AI-assisted search — built into every workflow
  • What's new: MCP — connect any external AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) directly to your ATS data
  • Built on the same open standard used by Salesforce, HubSpot, and Notion
  • Free add-on — included in your subscription, no consumption fees
  • You choose the AI. You own the workflow.
  • When a better AI model launches, you just plug it in

What MCP Adds On Top of Copilot

Copilot handles AI inside Teamtailor. MCP lets you bring your own AI tools to the table.

🔍
"Show me my open positions"

Ask ChatGPT or Claude to pull up all active jobs with status, applicant counts, and posting dates — without opening Teamtailor.

👤
"Summarize this candidate"

Get an AI summary of any candidate's application, resume, and notes — right in the AI tool you're already using.

✍️
"Update this job description"

Edit and publish job postings through your AI assistant without switching tabs.

📊
"How's our pipeline looking?"

Real-time pipeline analytics — who's in review, interviewing, or needs follow-up.

📋
"Find candidates for this role"

Your AI searches your existing talent pool and surfaces matches from past applicants.

🤖
"Run my morning briefing"

Set up a daily prompt that summarizes new applicants, upcoming interviews, and stale candidates.

Why This Matters

The AI model that's best today won't be best in 12 months. Your ATS architecture should account for that.

No Vendor Lock-In

MCP is an open standard — not proprietary tech. If you switch AI tools, your ATS connection stays the same.

You Own Your Workflows

Build AI workflows that match how you recruit — not how a vendor thinks you should.

Future-Proof by Design

When GPT-5 or Claude 5 launches, you connect it to Teamtailor the same day. No waiting, no upgrade fees.
